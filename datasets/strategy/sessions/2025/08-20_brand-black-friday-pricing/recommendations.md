# Strategic Recommendations: Brand Black Friday Unlimited Plan

**Session**: 2025-08-20 Brand Black Friday Pricing Strategy  
**Status**: Final Recommendations  
**Implementation Priority**: High (2-week launch target)

## Executive Summary

**Recommendation**: Launch tiered Brand Black Friday Unlimited Plan with aggressive $99/month pricing for small brands ($1-5M sales) and $299/month for medium brands ($5-15M sales). Include unlimited AI messages, email generation, and segmentation with 12-month commitment requirement.

**Strategic Rationale**: Mirrors successful agency Black Friday promotion while adapting to individual brand economics. Creates massive market penetration opportunity with sustainable unit economics and overwhelming competitive advantage.

## Final Pricing Structure

### Tier 1: Small Brands ($1-5M Annual Sales)
- **Price**: $99/month
- **Commitment**: 12-month annual commitment
- **Includes**: Unlimited AI messages + email generation + segmentation
- **Target Market**: 70% of promotion customers
- **Unit Economics**: 63-84% gross margins across usage scenarios

### Tier 2: Medium Brands ($5-15M Annual Sales)  
- **Price**: $299/month
- **Commitment**: 12-month annual commitment
- **Includes**: Unlimited AI messages + email generation + segmentation
- **Target Market**: 30% of promotion customers
- **Unit Economics**: 71-91% gross margins across usage scenarios

### Exclusions
- **Large Brands (>$15M sales)**: Refer to enterprise pricing
- **Monthly Plans**: Annual commitment required for promotional pricing
- **Usage Limits**: Fair use policy with monitoring for extreme usage
