# Strategic Decision Frameworks

## Common Frameworks for Different Decision Types

### Competitive Strategy Decisions
- **Porter's Five Forces**: Industry competition analysis
- **SWOT Analysis**: Strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, threats
- **Blue Ocean Strategy**: Value innovation and market creation

### Product Strategy Decisions  
- **Jobs-to-be-Done**: Customer outcome and job mapping
- **Product-Market Fit Canvas**: Market validation framework
- **ICE Scoring**: Impact, confidence, ease prioritization

### Pricing Strategy Decisions
- **Value-Based Pricing**: Customer willingness to pay analysis  
- **Competitive Pricing Analysis**: Market positioning framework
- **Cost-Plus Analysis**: Margin and profitability framework

### Market Entry Decisions
- **TAM/SAM/SOM Analysis**: Market sizing framework
- **Go-to-Market Strategy Canvas**: Channel and customer acquisition
- **Risk Assessment Matrix**: Market entry risk evaluation

### Growth Strategy Decisions
- **Ansoff Matrix**: Product/market growth options
- **Platform Strategy Framework**: Ecosystem and network effects
- **Unit Economics Analysis**: Scalability and profitability

## Framework Selection Guide

1. **Identify Decision Type**: What category does this decision fall into?
2. **Select Primary Framework**: Choose the most relevant framework
3. **Gather Required Data**: What research/data does the framework need?
4. **Apply Framework**: Work through the framework systematically  
5. **Cross-Check**: Use secondary framework to validate findings

## Decision Quality Checklist

- [ ] Clear problem statement defined
- [ ] Relevant stakeholders identified
- [ ] Sufficient research gathered  
- [ ] Multiple options considered
- [ ] Framework applied systematically
- [ ] Assumptions documented
- [ ] Success metrics defined
- [ ] Implementation plan created
